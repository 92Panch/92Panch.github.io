Welcome to the World of Tennis

Tennis is a dynamic and exciting sport that has captured the hearts of millions around the globe. On our website, you will find everything you need to know about the history of the game, key rules, and modern tournaments.

Fun Fact 1

Yellow tennis balls were first used at Wimbledon in 1986 to ensure they were more visible for television viewers.

Fun Fact 2

The fastest tennis serve ever recorded was hit by Australian player Samuel Groth in 2012, reaching a staggering speed of 163.4 mph (263 km/h)!
