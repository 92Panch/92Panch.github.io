How to Embed a YouTube Video

A step-by-step guide for your website.

Step 1: Search for a Video

Go to YouTube.com and use the search bar at the top of the page to find the video you want to embed. Press Enter or click the magnifying glass icon.

Step 2: Locate the Video

Once you find the right video in the search results, click on it. Below the video player, click the "Share" button.

Step 3: Select the "Embed" Option

A sharing menu will appear. Click on the "Embed" icon (represented by the < > symbols).

Step 4: Copy the HTML Code

A panel will open showing the <iframe> code. Click the "Copy" button in the bottom right corner of the window.

Step 5: Paste Code into your HTML

Go to your code editor and paste the copied snippet directly into your HTML file where you want the video to appear.
