Basic Tennis Rules

The goal of the game is to hit the ball over the net with a racket into the opponent's side of the court in a way that they cannot return it.

The Scoring System

Points within a single game are counted in a unique way: 15, 30, 40, and game.

  • First point - 15
  • Second point - 30
  • Third point - 40
  • Deuce - Occurs when the score reaches 40:40
